About

Obanai Iguro is the Serpent Hashira (蛇柱, Hebi Bashira). Stern, strict, and often harsh, Obanai nevertheless has a strong sense of justice and unwavering loyalty to the Demon Slayer Corps. His unique sword style mimics the slithering of snakes.
