About

Mitsuri Kanroji is the Love Hashira (恋柱, Koi Bashira). She is cheerful, kind, and emotional, yet possesses immense strength and unique sword techniques fueled by her passion. Her unusual muscular density makes her incredibly powerful despite her gentle appearance.
