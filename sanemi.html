Sanemi Shinazugawa (不死川 実弥)

About

Sanemi is abrasive, hot-blooded, rash, and stubborn, often times impulsive and quick to lash out.

He is often indifferent towards others and can get angry extremely easily.

Sanemi has also displayed a hunger for battle, wishing that he could encounter Upper Rank demons and proclaiming his excitement in fighting Upper Rank One, Kokushibo

Sanemi's cold, rude, and dismissive behavior is the result of losing too many people he cared about in the past.

Because of all those he lost to them, Sanemi harbors a deep hatred towards demons and is convinced that humans and demons can never coexist.

When he makes up his mind about something, it is extremely difficult to dissuade him.

Despite his poor treatment of his brother, Genya, Sanemi truly did love and care for his brother, admitting that he blames himself for not protecting him.
