About

Sanemi Shinazugawa is the Wind Hashira (風柱, Kaze Bashira). Hot-headed and brash, Sanemi has a deep hatred for demons. Despite his abrasive personality, he is one of the strongest Hashira, known for his resilience, ferocity, and powerful wind techniques.
