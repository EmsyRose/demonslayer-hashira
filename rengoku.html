About

Kyojuro Rengoku is the Flame Hashira (炎柱, Enbashira) of the Demon Slayer Corps. Known for his passionate spirit, unwavering sense of justice, and burning determination, Rengoku embodies the brightness and strength of flame. He is a mentor and source of inspiration to many Demon Slayers.
