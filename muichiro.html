About

Muichiro Tokito is the Mist Hashira (霞柱, Kasumi Bashira). Despite his airheaded and aloof nature, Muichiro is a genius swordsman who became a Hashira in just two months of training. He is deeply connected to the legacy of the Demon Slayer Corps.
