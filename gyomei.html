About

Gyomei Himejima is the Stone Hashira (岩柱, Iwa Bashira). Despite his blindess, Gyomei is considered the strongest Hashira in the Demon Slayer Corps. A kind soul who weeps easily, he is nonetheless a towering and fearsome warrior in battle.
