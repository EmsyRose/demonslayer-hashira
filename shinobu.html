About

Shinobu Kocho is the Insect Hashira (蟲柱, Mushibashira) of the Demon Slayer Corps. Known for her graceful yet deadly fighting style, she uses poison-coated blades to slay demons. Despite her soft-spoken and kind demeanor, Shinobu harbors a deep hatred for demons due to her family’s tragic fate.
