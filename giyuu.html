About

Giyu Tomioka is the Water Hashira (水柱, Mizubashira) of the Demon Slayer Corps. Calm, stoic, and powerful, he is a master of the Water Breathing techniques. Though reserved, he is deeply compassionate and plays a vital role in guiding Tanjiro and Nezuko.
