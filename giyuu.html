Giyuu Tomioka (冨岡 義勇)

About

Giyuu Tomioka is the Water Hashira, a man reserved and stoic, who plays a major supporting role in Kimetsu No Yaiba.

Due to his aloof demeanor, Giyuu is not close with the other Hashira, and is often hated.
