About

Tengen Uzui is the Sound Hashira (音柱, Oto Bashira) of the Demon Slayer Corps. Known for his flamboyant and “flashy” personality, he values extravagance and style even in battle. A former shinobi, Tengen combines speed, strength, and sound techniques to overwhelm his enemies.
